About Us

Team Barcelona was established in 2008 by a couple of parents who got together to coach theirs sons and their sons' friends. They started playing on their neighborhood and decided to form a team and started participating in local leagues. At the beginning they were losing all their games but soon thereafter started to get better and better and now they are a very competitive team playing at the Division 2 level.
